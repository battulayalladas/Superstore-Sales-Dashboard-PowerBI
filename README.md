# Superstore-Sales-Dashboard-PowerBI
Power BI dashboard analyzing retail sales, profit, and YoY trends across categories, regions, and shipping modes.
# 🛍️ Super Store Sales Dashboard | Power BI

## Business Problem
A retail superstore had no visibility into which 
categories, regions, and shipping modes drive 
maximum sales and profit.

## Tools Used
- Power BI (Dashboard & Visualizations)

## Visuals Created
- KPI Cards: Total Sales, Total Profit
- Sales by Category & Sub-Category
- Sales by Shipping Mode
- Monthly Sales & Profit YoY Trend
- Region-wise Performance

## Key Insights
- Identified top performing product categories
- Monthly Year-over-Year sales growth tracked
- Shipping mode impact on revenue analyzed
- Regional sales comparison for strategy
